### **Summary:**

The **Darc Incheon Website** was developed as a freelance project for the **Incheon Drug Addiction Rehabilitation Center (DARC)**, part of the **Darc Community**, a non-profit organization that supports individuals recovering from drug addiction. This project was undertaken entirely by me, from initial design to deployment, showcasing my ability to deliver a professional, fully functional platform tailored to the client’s needs.

#### **About the Darc Community**:
The **Darc Community** is dedicated to drug addiction rehabilitation, creating a safe and nurturing environment for individuals to rebuild their lives. The Incheon chapter focuses on education, therapy, and empowerment through group programs, workshops, and community events. This website plays a crucial role in advancing their mission by streamlining resource access and enhancing public awareness.

---

### **My Role**:
As a freelance developer, I was responsible for:
- **End-to-End Development**:
  - Designed and implemented the website from scratch, including frontend, backend, and database integration.
- **Feature Implementation**:
  - Developed critical functionalities such as volunteer forms, donation handling, and appointment scheduling.
- **Admin Panel**:
  - Built a secure admin interface with role-based access for managing multimedia and content updates.
- **SEO Optimization**:
  - Ensured the site ranks well on Google and Naver by optimizing metadata, generating sitemaps, and integrating social tags.
- **Deployment**:
  - Hosted the platform on **Vercel**, leveraging its seamless integration with Next.js for smooth performance and scalability.
- **Client Communication**:
  - Worked directly with the organization to gather requirements, provide progress updates, and ensure alignment with their goals.

---

### **Core Features**:
- **Volunteer Management**:
  - Allows users to submit applications with automated email notifications sent to administrators.
- **Donation System**:
  - Secure payment integration for accepting and tracking donations.
- **Appointment Scheduling**:
  - A backend-powered scheduler for event and consultation bookings.
- **Admin Panel**:
  - Secure login (using bcrypt encryption) for authorized personnel to manage videos, announcements, and text data.
- **Responsive Design**:
  - Ensures seamless usability across all devices, enhancing user engagement.
- **SEO Optimization**:
  - Tailored for Google and Naver to improve the site's visibility and reach.

---

### **Tech Stack**

#### **Frontend**:
- **Next.js** for server-side rendering (SSR) and static site generation (SSG).
- **Traditional CSS** for responsive and user-friendly design.

#### **Backend**:
- **Next.js API Routes** for RESTful API development.
- **MongoDB** for storing and managing user and event data.

#### **Deployment**:
- Hosted on **Vercel**, enabling easy scaling and high availability.

#### **Additional Features**:
- **Authentication**:
  - Secure admin access using **bcrypt** encryption.
- **SEO Optimization**:
  - Includes dynamic metadata, sitemaps, and sharing tags.
