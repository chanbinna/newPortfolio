### **Summary**  
As Vice President of **Likelion UCSD**, I spearheaded the creation of the **Likelion UCSD Beginner Project Website**, designed to teach web development fundamentals through structured lessons and projects. This platform supports students in gaining hands-on experience with modern web technologies while serving as an educational hub.

#### **About Likelion UCSD**  
**Likelion UCSD** is a chapter of the international **Likelion** organization, which empowers students to transform their ideas into tangible digital products. At UCSD, the focus is on providing beginner-friendly web development education through collaborative projects and a strong support network. The organization aims to bridge the gap between studentsâ€™ aspirations and technical capabilities by fostering a vibrant community of learners and builders.

#### **Key Contributions**  
- **Comprehensive Curriculum Development**: Designed a step-by-step curriculum that includes hands-on projects to teach core web development technologies such as **HTML**, **CSS**, **JavaScript**, **React**, **Next.js**, **GraphQL**, and **MongoDB**.  
- **Website Development**: Built the website as a platform to host lessons, showcase student projects, and provide educational resources.  
- **Project-Based Learning**: Developed project examples for students to apply their skills in building full-stack web applications.  
- **Team Leadership**: Recruited and led project teams, managing roles, resolving conflicts, and ensuring deadlines were met.  
- **Student Mentorship**: Delivered weekly workshops, mentoring beginners on technical challenges and best practices.  
- **Deployment & Maintenance**: Deployed the platform on **Vercel**, ensuring optimal performance, scalability, and accessibility.
