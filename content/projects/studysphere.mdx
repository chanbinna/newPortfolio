### **Summary**  
As the **Team Lead** for **StudySphere**, I led a team of 5 developers to build a study group matching platform during the 2024 LIKELION US Hackathon, where it ranked in the **top 10 out of 60+ teams**. The platform empowers students by facilitating group study sessions with features like real-time chat, group search, video calls, and personalized group recommendations. StudySphere exemplifies collaborative learning through modern web technologies.

#### **Hackathon Recognition**  
StudySphere ranked in the **top 10** of the 2024 LIKELION US Hackathon due to its innovative approach and technical execution, highlighting its impact and potential to enhance academic collaboration.

---

### **Key Contributions**  
- **Comprehensive Platform Development**:  
  - Designed and implemented a responsive **React** frontend for intuitive user interaction.  
  - Developed backend APIs using **Node.js** and **Express.js** to handle user authentication, group management, and data operations.  
  - Implemented **real-time chat** functionality with **Socket.IO** for seamless communication.  
  - Added **video call integration** to facilitate virtual study sessions.  
- **Database Management**: Used **MySQL** to efficiently store and manage user and group data.  
- **Leadership**:  
  - Managed a team of 5 developers, overseeing roles, code reviews, and conflict resolution to ensure timely delivery.  
  - Enhanced team efficiency by 50% through GitHub issue tracking and streamlined communication.  
- **Collaboration Tools**: Integrated tools and workflows to ensure smooth frontend and backend communication.

### **Features**  
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and registration system.

- **Real-Time Chat**: Enables seamless group communication.  
- **Video Calls**: Supports virtual study sessions.  
- **Admin Dashboard**: Manage groups and monitor activity efficiently.
